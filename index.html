About SMCC

Learn more about our vision, mission, goal, and objectives.

Vision

Saint Michael College of Caraga envisions to be a University by 2035 and upholds spiritual formation and excellence in teaching, service, and research.

Mission

  • SMCC shall provide spiritual formation and learning culture that ensures students excellent learning experiences.
  • Engage in dynamic and interdisciplinary research.
  • Serve diverse communities through service-learning.

Goal

Uphold Culture of Excellence in the Areas of Spiritual Formation, Instruction, Research, and Extension.

General Objectives

  • Integrate evangelical values and design responsive formation programs.
  • Continuously enhance the curriculum and teaching effectiveness.
  • Upgrade facilities and services.
  • Conduct dynamic and innovative research.
  • Inspire learners to become socially responsible.
  • Provide holistic student services.
  • Establish strong stakeholder partnerships.
  • Work toward recognized accreditation.
